Testing Instructions

Guide for reviewers or developers testing Shudder Files integrations.

Setup

  1. Log in using a test business or creator account with appropriate publishing permissions.
  2. Grant access when prompted by the OAuth login window.
  3. After authorization, your access token will be used to verify posting permissions.

Testing Auto Publishing

  • Create a short test video or image for posting.
  • Submit the media through the app dashboard or API endpoint.
  • Confirm that the content appears correctly on the connected profile or page.

Troubleshooting

If publishing fails, verify your permissions, token validity, and connected account roles. For support, email shudder.files@outlook.com.
