Data Deletion

Instructions for users who wish to delete their information.

If you have used the Shudder Files automation tools and would like to delete your account data, publishing logs, or integration history, we offer a simple and compliant way to proceed.

Request Deletion

  1. Email us at shudder.files@outlook.com using the same email associated with your account or OAuth login.
  2. Use the subject line: “Data Deletion Request”.
  3. If possible, include any relevant platform IDs (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook) or integration details.

Once confirmed, all associated data will be permanently deleted within 72 hours. You will receive a confirmation email upon completion.

What is Deleted

  • OAuth tokens and access credentials
  • Publishing history, post metadata, analytics cache
  • Any linked email addresses or temporary logs
