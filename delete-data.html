Data Deletion

Instructions for users who wish to delete their information.

If you have used Shudder Files and would like your account data or publishing information removed, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Data Deletion

  1. Send an email to shudder.files@outlook.com using the same address linked to your account.
  2. Include the subject line: “Data Deletion Request”.
  3. Specify any relevant IDs or platform usernames if applicable.

Your data will be permanently deleted from our servers and integrations within 72 hours of confirmation.

Note

We do not retain content, credentials, or media after deletion requests are completed.
