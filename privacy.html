Privacy Policy

How we collect, store, and use your information.

TechTupa, registered as MEI (CNPJ: 62.598.036/0001-76, Brazil) and operated by Hugo Moraes, is responsible for the Shudder Files and AutoPublish applications. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This document explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is handled.

Information We Collect

  • Basic account details (name, email) when authorized through OAuth integrations.
  • Video metadata such as captions, IDs, timestamps, and descriptions for automated uploads.
  • Temporary authentication tokens issued by TikTok or Meta for publishing automation.
  • Technical logs used solely for performance monitoring and troubleshooting.

How We Use the Information

The collected information is used exclusively to enable secure video publishing, manage metadata, and provide support for the AutoPublish system. No personal data is sold, traded, or shared with third parties. Access tokens are temporary and automatically expire.

Data Retention

Data is stored only as long as required to operate the service. Users can request data deletion at any time by contacting shudder.files@outlook.com. Upon revocation of TikTok or Meta authorization, all access is immediately removed.

Security

We follow industry-standard practices to secure stored information, including encrypted HTTPS communication, limited access, and secure server environments. TechTupa does not store passwords, personal messages, or any sensitive user content.
