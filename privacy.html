Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

TechTupa values privacy and transparency. This application does not store, sell, or share any personal data from users. Any access to your TikTok account occurs solely through the official TikTok API and requires explicit user authorization.

Information obtained through authentication tokens is used exclusively to automate video uploads and related metadata management for the Shudder Files channel. Tokens are securely stored in the creator’s own environment (such as n8n or a connected VPS) and can be revoked at any time from TikTok’s security settings.

For privacy inquiries, please contact: support@techtupa.com.br
