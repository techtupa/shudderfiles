Privacy Policy

How we collect, store, and use your information.

At Shudder Files, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This document outlines what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Basic account details such as name or email when provided through integrations.
  • Content metadata (e.g., post ID, caption, timestamps) for automated publishing.
  • Technical logs required for app operation and performance analysis.

How We Use the Information

Information is used exclusively to enable automated publishing, analytics, and support functionalities across connected platforms. We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.

Data Retention

All data is retained only for the duration necessary to provide the service. Users can request data deletion at any time via shudder.files@outlook.com.

Security

We apply industry-standard security measures to protect stored information, including encrypted communication and secure access control.

Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at shudder.files@outlook.com.
